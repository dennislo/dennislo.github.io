Software Engineering is concerned with the design and implementation of reliable and secure software systems, which have
the functionality required for the intended purpose. Software Engineering is also sometimes referred to as "software
programming". The tech giant Bill Gates started programming software when he was in High School - his hobby has built
one of the biggest software companies in the world. Software Engineering is a booming industry with no shortage of
opportunities to create unique and life changing programs.
