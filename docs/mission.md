# Mission

This is a personal website built with Gatsby, React, and TypeScript.

## Why

This site exists to share Dennis Lo's work, ideas, and experiments through a fast, maintainable personal website built
with Gatsby, React, and TypeScript. It serves as a professional home on the web where writing, projects, and thoughtful
frontend craftsmanship can live together in one place.

## Audiences

The primary audience is people who want to learn more about Dennis, including potential collaborators, employers,
clients, and peers in the software community. A secondary audience is fellow developers who may benefit from the site's
technical implementation, testing practices, and overall approach to building a polished personal web presence.
